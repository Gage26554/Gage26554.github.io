Rpg Maker Mv

Rpg maker Mv is one of the coolest things that we did in my opinion. We were making a game! You can't tell me that's cool. With Rpg maker we learned about flowcharts, variables, and switchs! And we used that to make our own game pretty cool man. With switches we were able to create a market stall stealing event where if you got caught it activated a switch that made it so the seller would have guards attack you.
